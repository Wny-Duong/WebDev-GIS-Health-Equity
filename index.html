Welcome to the West LA Food Empowerment Project!

The Covid-19 pandemic has brought about many food-related challenges for the residents of West Los Angeles. We want to acknowledge that some may have experience a decline in food security during this time.

Here are some things we are aiming to achieve through this website:

  1. Document and Create a safe space for community members of West LA to share their experiences and challenges while applying to food aid programs
  2. Allow users of food aid services in West LA to provide feedback and suggest improvements to food aid services.
  3. Allow residents of West LA to learn about the food aid programs offered in their local area

This website displays information that we collect through a survey. If you would like to participate in the survey and tell your story about how COVID-19 has impacted your relationship to food security, please click on the "Take Survey" button below. Otherwise, click on the "Go To Map" or X button to proceed to our website.
